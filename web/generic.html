Effects of Climate Change

Every minute that we do not care about the world, climate change is having visible effects on the our planet.

Some Changes in the Climate System and the Impacts of Them

It gives damage in lots of ways such as some changes in the hydrological cycle, melting of the icebergs, warming oceans, rising sea levels, unexpected and extreme weather conditions. These changings have significant impacts on climate change. Some of them are decreasing water supplies, fisheries failing, heat stress, decreasing of food supplies, loss of biodiversity, damage to the forests and the animals which are living in them.
